CAMBIO

Difficulty:

How to Play

Draw a card from the deck or discard pile each turn. Swap it with one of your face-down cards, or discard it.
Call Cambio instead of drawing — everyone else gets one final turn, then lowest score wins.

Special cards (only trigger when discarded from deck draw):
10 → Peek at one of your own cards
J → Blind swap one of your cards with opponent's
Q → Peek at one of the opponent's cards
K → Peek opponent's card, optionally swap

Snap: After any discard, click one of your own cards if it matches the discarded value — correct snap removes it from your hand. Wrong snap adds 2 penalty cards. You cannot snap the card you just put down.

Scoring: A=1 · 2–9=face value · 10/J/Q/K=10 · K♥=−1 · Joker=0. Lowest total wins.
