CAMBIO

How to play:
Draw a card from the deck or discard pile each turn. Swap it with one of your face-down cards, or discard it.
Call Cambio instead of drawing — everyone else gets one final turn, then lowest score wins.

Special cards (trigger when discarded):
10 → Peek at one of your own cards  |  J → Blind swap one of your cards with opponent's  |  Q → Peek at one of the opponent's cards  |  K → Peek opponent's card, optionally swap

Scoring: A=1, 2–9=face value, 10/J/Q/K=10, K♥=−1, Joker=0. Lowest total wins.
